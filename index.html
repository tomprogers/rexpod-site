rexpod

regular expressions + Scrabble dictionary

rexpod is the iOS app that searches the Scrabble dictionary using Javascript Regular Expressions.

Getting started

  1. Type a regular expression into the text box at the top of the screen.
  2. Press "Search" on the iPhone keyboard.
  3. After a short delay, all the English words that match your regular expression will appear below the keyboard. These are your seaerch results.
  4. You may tap any of the search results to highlight it. Highlighting a word has no other effect, it's just a kind of visual bookmark.

Getting help

  • For information about Javascript Regular Expressions, see the Mozilla Developer Center's Regular Expressions page.
